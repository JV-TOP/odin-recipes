Mixed Sandwich

If you would like to have a quick dinner,
look no further. The ultimate speed is 
in making a sandwich!
This recipe focus on using big quantities
of ham and melted cheese. It could be the
perfect combination for a flat white,
or for a milkshake.
        
            
Preparation: 6 min
Cooking: 4 minutes
Quantity: 2 Units
Difficulty: Low

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 spoons of butter
  • 4 slices of emmental cheese
  • 6 thin slices of ham

Steps:

  1. Spread the butter along both the external surfaces of the bread.
  2. Place a slice of emmental cheese on top of each bread slice.
  3. Add the ham on top of each slice of cheese.
  4. Add another cheese slice on top of the ham slices.
  5. Close the sandwich with a slice of bread. The sandwich will end up arranged in the following order: bread + cheese + ham + cheese + bread.
  6. Put the sandwich in the toaster, sandwich maker or at slow fire on the saucepan for about 2 minutes per each sandwich.
  7. Serve and enjoy.
