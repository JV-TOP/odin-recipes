Reina Pepiada

Source: Recetavenezolana.com

Chicken-Avocado deli arepa

Reina Pepiada (old Venezuelan Spanish 
for "curvy queen") is a filling for 
arepa composed of avocado, chicken, 
and mayonnaise.
This particular filling is named 
after the Venezuelan beauty queen 
Susana Duijm.
        
            
Preparation: 15 min
Cooking: 23 minutes
Quantity: 4 Arepas
Difficulty: Low

Ingredients:

  • 4 recently made, hot arepas.
  • 2 chicken breasts.
  • 500 gr. of avocado.
  • 1/4 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 pinch of black pepper

Steps:

  1. Boil the chicken without the skin
  2. Wait for them to cool down until you can use your hands to divide it into small pieces, and put them in a small container
  3. Cut the avocado in small pieces and mix it with the chicken
  4. Add the mayonnaise, salt and pepper to the mix
  5. with the mix obtained, fill the arepas
