Eggs Benedict

A dish considered Gourmet due to the combination
of its ingredients (poached eggs, hollandaise, 
and crunchy bacon). Eggs benedict are considered
one of the most popular dishes in US and England.
        
            
Preparation: 5 min
Cooking: 13 minutes
Quantity: 1 person
Difficulty: Low

Ingredients:

  • 1 slice of white loaf
  • 125 gr. of Butter
  • 2 eggs yellow at room temperature for the sauce
  • 1 tbsp. of lemon juice
  • 1 tsp. of salt
  • 1/2 tsp. of black pepper
  • 3 slices of bacon
  • 1/2 liter of water for poaching the eggs
  • 1/4 cup of vinegar
  • 1 egg

Steps:

  1. Toast the slice of bread that would serve as the base. Hold onto it for now in a separate plate.
  2. Using a slow fire, melt the butter in a pot.
  3. Wait until a foam appears on the surface. This is the buttermilk that needs to be removed. It will take approximately 5 minutes.
  4. Remove all the foam and the impurities that can be located in the bottom and leave only the fat.
  5. Take the two egg yellows and mix in a bowl.
  6. Slowly add the butter over the mixed eggs and create a emulsion by shaking with a stick, until it becomes creamy.
  7. Add the lemon juice, salt and pepper then mix again to integrate. Put apart.
  8. Warm the water with vinegar.
  9. Using a spoon make a specie of whirlpool in the water, then add the egg.
  10. Allow it to simmer for 5 minutes.
  11. With a lot of care, remove the egg from the water and allow it to dry over kitchen paper.
  12. Put together the dish starting by the toast bread, crunchy bacon, poached egg and hollandaise sauce above it.
  13. Serve and enjoy.
